3 Disc Tower of Hanoi puzzle

* Rules
        * ONLY THE TOP MOST DISC OF A TOWER CAN BE PLACED ON A TOP MOST DISC OF ANOTHER TOWER
        * ANY LARGER DISC CANNOT BE PUT ON TOP OF A SMALLER DISC
        
How to Win? - Move all discs to another tower
